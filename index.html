My work spans fair link prediction, multicalibration, combinatorial methods for trustworthy AI, and Boolean/function-theoretic approaches to GNN expressivity.

About

I work at the intersection of machine learning theory and socially responsible AI. My research explores how to design learning systems that remain accurate, fair, robust, and theoretically interpretable when deployed on structured and heterogeneous data.

I am especially interested in fairness under overlapping or intersectional groups, calibration and multicalibration, graph-based learning problems, and optimisation-based formulations of trustworthy decision-making.
